The Studio

Architectural rigor meets creative vision.

Who We Are

Asplan Associates is a multi-disciplinary practice born from the belief that exceptional architecture requires more than just design—it requires a deep mastery of planning policy and surveying.

Based in Dorset, we have spent years navigating the complex regulatory landscape of the South Coast, turning "difficult" plots into award-winning realities.

M

Our Mission

To bridge the divide between creative ambition and regulatory reality. We strive to unlock the full potential of every site through a unified approach to architecture, planning, and surveying, ensuring that our clients' visions are not just designed, but delivered.

V

Our Vision

To be the definitive architectural authority on the South Coast. We envision a built environment where sustainability, luxury, and functionality coexist, setting a new standard for how modern homes interact with their landscape.

E

Our Ethos

Our work is defined by an unshakeable commitment to three core values:

  • Precision in Detail
  • Integrity in Planning
  • Innovation in Design
