Heritage & Conservation

Preserving the narrative of the past while adapting for the future.
Specialists in Grade I & II Listed Buildings and Conservation Areas.

01. Research & Recording

Every historic building tells a story. We begin with detailed heritage impact assessments and archival research to understand the significance of the fabric we are touching.

02. Sensitive Intervention

Our philosophy is "minimum intervention, maximum impact." We use traditional materials—lime, stone, oak—alongside reversible modern techniques to protect the building's integrity.

03. Adaptive Reuse

The best way to save a building is to give it a purpose. We specialize in converting barns, churches, and derelict structures into exceptional modern spaces.
